Cultus is a blend of space and craft. Its trim body conceals ample space, flexibility for both passengers and storage. Suzuki Cultus assures everyone, exceptional value and quality. The Suzuki Cultus is the JDM version of the Suzuki Swift. Typical of many other JDM vehicles, many Cultus models had unique features like all wheel drive and higher powered engines, features not available in any other foreign market versions.

Price: $10,200

Engine: New G108 EFi 993cc

Transmission: Manual

Braking System: Ventilated Discs, Drum

Fuel: 40L (Patrol), 55L (CNG)

Steering: Rack-and-pinion

Available Colors: Solid White, Medium Silver, Black, White, Strong Blue, Gun Metallic, Silver

Audio: Audio with CD Player/AM-FM/MP3.

Safety: Driver: 3 Point ELR, with Pretensioner and Force Limiter Passenger.

Tire and Wheel: R15 Alloy Rims.

Air Conditioning System: Electric Air Condition System.
